Privacy Policy for Snackdown

Last Updated: January 1, 2026

Overview

Snackdown ("the Game") is developed by Rick Brandmeier. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

Leaderboard Data

  • Player Name: The display name you choose for the leaderboard
  • Score: Your survival time in the game
  • Character Used: Which character you played as

This information is stored on Firebase servers to display global leaderboards.

Device Information

We use Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Purchase transactions are processed by Apple. We do not store your payment information. See Apple's Privacy Policy for details.

How We Use Your Information

  • Display your scores on the global leaderboard
  • Show relevant advertisements
  • Process in-app purchases

Data Retention

Leaderboard data is retained indefinitely to maintain historical rankings. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Snackdown is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Third-Party Services

  • Firebase/Firestore: Cloud database for leaderboards (Privacy Policy)
  • Google AdMob: Advertising (Privacy Policy)
  • Apple: In-app purchases and App Store (Privacy Policy)

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page.
