About the Game

Snackdown is a Vampire Survivors-style survival game where you fight endless waves of food-themed enemies!

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play?

  • Use the joystick to move your character
  • Weapons fire automatically at nearby enemies
  • Collect XP orbs to level up and get upgrades
  • Survive as long as possible!

How do I change characters?

Tap on a different character on the main menu before starting a game.

How do I get more Chef's Hats?

  • Earn them by playing (1 Chef's Hat per 10 seconds survived)
  • Purchase 1,000 Chef's Hats in the Shop

My game is crashing, what do I do?

  1. Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
  2. Try restarting your device
  3. If the issue persists, please contact us (see below)

How do I report a bug?

Email us with:

  • A description of the bug
  • What you were doing when it happened
  • Your device model and iOS version
